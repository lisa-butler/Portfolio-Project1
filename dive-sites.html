Shore Dives in Donegal

Malin Beg

  • Dive Type: Shore
  • Dive Skill: Easy
  • Acessibility: Steps
  • Depth: 4-18m

  • How to dive:

    Channel runs East-West. Swim to the stack on shore and drop at the stack. A passable through cave is present on the right side of the bay on the way out, slightly further out than the stack.

  • Specific Interest:

    Sea stack, cave, octopus.

  • Best time to dive:

    Mid tide, quite sheltered. Night dive for octopus.
